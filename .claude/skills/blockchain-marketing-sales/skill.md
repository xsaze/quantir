### 2. Implementation Patterns

**Airdrop Allocation Formulas:**
- **Tiered Flat:** Set amounts per tier (e.g., 400 UNI minimum)
- **Activity-Weighted:** Score based on actions (Optimism: delegation-days × multiplier)

- **Quadratic:** Benefits early/small participants (Gitcoin model)

### 6. Security & Production

- ✅ Comprehensive audit (code + modules + proxies + integrations)
- ✅ Multi-sig wallet (minimum 3-of-5 for token distribution)
- ✅ Time locks on critical functions
- ✅ Rate limiting on claims
- ✅ Reentrancy guards (CEI pattern)
- ✅ Gas optimization (~60k gas savings possible per claim)
- ✅ Testnet deployment (7+ days operation)
- ✅ Load testing (10k concurrent users)

- **Chainalysis:** Fraud detection (enterprise)

- Claim rate (% of eligible addresses claiming)

## Key Insights & Principles

### Web3 Marketing Paradigm Shift

**Web2 → Web3 Differences:**
- Data ownership: Company → User
- Relationship: Customer → Stakeholder
- Distribution: Platform → Protocol
- Incentives: Engagement → Economic alignment
- Metrics: Pageviews → On-chain activity

**2025 Market Context:**
- $12B global Web3 advertising spend (40% YoY growth)
- $250B+ TVL in DeFi (precision marketing required)
- 70% airdrop waste without anti-Sybil (CRITICAL ISSUE)
- Quest platforms: 10-20x higher engagement vs traditional
- Community-first: 3-5x better long-term ROI

### Critical Success Factors

**1. Anti-Sybil Protection is NON-NEGOTIABLE**
- 70% token waste without protection
- Layered verification required for >$50K campaigns
- Budget 10-20% of token allocation for verification

**2. Community > Everything**
- Highest long-term ROI (2-8x)
- Sustainable growth (3-6 months to scale)
- 40-70% retention vs 15-30% for paid

**3. Security Must Be Proactive**
- $3.1B lost in H1 2025
- Smart contract audits mandatory
- Multi-sig wallets (minimum 3-of-5)
- Real-time monitoring essential

**4. Budget Optimization by Stage**
- Pre-launch: 60% community, 30% content
- Launch: 35% paid, 30% influencer, 20% quest
- Growth: 30% community, 25% paid, 20% quest
- Maturity: Community-first + partnerships

**5. Regulatory Compliance**
- $8.2B in SEC penalties (2024)
- Disclose all influencer payments
- Geo-blocking for restricted jurisdictions
- Legal review before launch

### Tradeoffs to Consider

**Speed vs Sustainability:**
- Fast (Paid/Influencer): Immediate, expensive, high churn
- Slow (Community/Content): 3-6 month lag, compounding, sustainable

**Cost vs Quality:**
- High CAC ($20-$100): Medium quality, 15-30% retention
- Low CAC ($5-$25 or $0): High quality, 40-70% retention

**Control vs Authenticity:**
- High control (Paid): Predictable, transactional feel
- Low control (Influencer/Community): Authentic, unpredictable

**Scale vs Engagement:**
- High scale: Millions reached, 1-5% engagement, bot risk
- High engagement: Smaller numbers, 15-40% engagement, genuine
