## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### Twitter/X
- Post frequency: 3-5 times daily
- Best times: 8-10 AM, 2-4 PM, 8-10 PM (user's timezone)
- Hashtags: 3-5 per post, mix popular + niche
- Media: Always include image/GIF/video
- Engagement: Reply within 15 minutes

### Telegram
- Admin activity: 24/7 coverage (rotating mods)
- Rules enforcement: Immediate for scams/spam
- Engagement: Daily polls, trivia, discussions
- Announcements: Pin important messages
- Voice chats: Weekly minimum for Q&A

### Discord
- Channel organization: Clear categories, purpose
- Role distribution: Reward engagement
- Bot integration: Welcome, moderation, utility
- Events: Weekly voice/text events
- Content: Exclusive for Discord members

### Reddit
- Subreddit creation: Once 1000+ holders
- Moderation: Strict against pump/dump
- Content: Educational, memes, discussions
- AMAs: Monthly with team
- Cross-promotion: Carefully, avoid spam

### TikTok
- Short format: 15-60 second clips
- Trends: Participate in relevant challenges
- Education: Bite-sized "how to buy" guides
- Personality: Show team, community highlights
- Music: Trending audio for algorithm boost
